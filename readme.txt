Publish fact-checking articles on your platform

== Description ==
Create an publish fact-checking articles on your platform, with the possibility to other useful information tu the article, like the claim, its source, the verdict and the explainer. The added information will be displayed above the article content to guide the readers, and will also be used to add structured data to the article.

== Installation ==
From your admin dashboard, go to 'Plugins > Add New', then in the research bar, search for WP Factcheck. When the plugin appears, click on the Install button. The plugin will install. When it is done, click on the 'Activate' button.

You can also download the plugin zip, and upload it. From the 'Plugin > Add New' page, click on the 'Upload Plugin' button. An upload field appears. Select the zip file from your computer and click on 'Upload'. The zip will be uploaded, and the plugin installed.

Activate the plugin and start publishing your fact-checking articles.
